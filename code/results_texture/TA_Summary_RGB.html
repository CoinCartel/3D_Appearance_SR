The whole dataset contains 24 texture maps, including 13 from ETH3D, 6 from Collection, 3 from SyB3R, and 2 from MiddleBury. 

 
Description of learning-based method.
EDSR_T: 		directly testing on the pretrained EDSR model.

EDSR_WON: 		finetuning on texture maps, without the normal and mask information. The 24 texture maps
 			are split into two subsets and each of then contains 12 images. When one subset is used 
 			for training, the other is used for test. Texture maps from each dataset ETH3D, Collection, 
 			MiddleBury, and SyB3R are equally assigned to the split subset. 100 epochs are used.

EDSR_W: 		NLR method reported in CVPR19 paper. finetuning on texture maps, with the normal and mask information. 
 			The same dataset splitting method with EDSR_WON is used. 100 epochs are used.

EDSR_WON_Solo:		For each of the four subsets ETH3D, Collection, MiddleBury, and SyB3R, dataset splitting 
			is implemented within the subset. Training and testing use images from the same subset. Take ETH3D
			for example. The 13 texture maps are split into two subsets with 6 and 7 images respectively. When 
			on is used for training, the other is used for testing. In this way, the other three subsets are 
			not involved in the training  and testing of ETH3D. 50 epochs are used. 
		
EDSR_WN_Solo: 		The same setups with EDSR_WON_Solo. But normal and mask information are used.

EDSR_WN_Solo_E100:	The same setups with EDSR_WN_Solo. 100 epochs are used.

The used normal information in the above method are all LR normal maps. The network architecture only includes 
32 Residual Blocks. This is different from the method below such as EDSR_NORMAL_HR, and EDSR_NORMAL_LR that not
only has 32 Residual Blocks but also include 4 additional convs in the tail part of the network.

EDSR_NORMAL_HR:		NHR method reported in CVPR19 paper. There are 32 Residual Blocks in the body part and additional
			4 convs in the tail part.

EDSR_NORMAL_LR:		Similar architecture as NHR. 32 Residual Blocks and 4 convs in the tail part.

EDSR_Res32:		Similar architecture as NLR without the 4 additional convs in the tail part.

HRTM2014:		Vagia's method. HRST reported in the paper.

HRTM2014_Post_Joint:	HRST-CNN reported in the paper.
